The Kenya Accountants and Secretaries National Examinations Board (KASNEB) is a professional examinations body tasked with the responsibility of developing syllabuses and at the same time conducting professional examinations. The body also certifies candidates in accountancy, finance, credit, governance and management, and information technology among other disciplines. The KASNEB contacts provided can be used for any queries regarding services offered by the body.

KASNEB is responsible for certification of candidates in accounts, finance, credit, governance, management, information technology and many other disciplines. Despite this many people lack KASNEB Contacts. The body has devoted its resources to ensure qualifications are promoted nationally and internationally. Using its authority, the body is involved in the accreditation of relevant training institutions under the guidance of the ministry of education. The exams offered by KASNEB include CPA (Certified Public Accountant), Credit Management Technicians (CMT) Examinations, Accounting Technicians Certificate (ATC) Examinations, Certified Credit Professional (CCP) Examinations, Certified Information Communication Technologists (CICT) Examination, Certified Public Secretaries (CPS) Examinations, and Investment and Security Technician (IST).

Many people usually question themselves whether there is a KASNEB cpa contact. Such questions can now be easily answered in all Huduma centers countrywide. Below are various branches of KASNEB offices, their customer care contacts and a guide on how to contact KASNEB Kenya.

KASNEB head office

The head office for KASNEB Kenya which serves as the headquarters of the body is located in Nairobi. Their offices are found in KASNEB Towers that is off the Hospital Road. It is located in close proximity with the Kenyatta National Hospital and next to Real Insurance. KASNEB office contacts and location of the headquarters are provided below.

Postal Address: P.O. Box 41362 00100 Nairobi Kenya

Telephone Numbers: Tel: 020 2712640, 020 2712828

ISDN: 020 4923000

Email: Info@KASNEB.or.ke

From the city center, you can get to KASNEB office by boarding bus number 7c in Kencom stage or use the KASNEB contacts Nairobi region to ask for directions.
