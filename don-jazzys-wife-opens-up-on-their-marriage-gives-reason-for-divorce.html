Don Jazzy's Wife Opens Up On Their Marriage, Gives Reason For Divorce

April 14, 2024
Post

Michelle Jackson, the ex-wife of Music promoter Don Jazzy has opened up on their marriage and given the reason for their divorce.

Michelle revealed in an interview with Pulse that despite occasional arguments and disagreements her marriage to Don Jazzy was enjoyable.

She explained that the marriage was bound to fail because they were too young and lacked experience.

She explained that the Main record boss has a very down to earth personality and they glowed together.

According to her: ”We were so carefree and happy, I don’t think we had so much baggage that maybe I would have now.

“It was just fun and of course every relationship has its ups and downs. We’d probably fight a while and then we would be cool again.

“Honestly I know that at that particular age, there were so many things that could have gone wrong, but for the most part of our marriage a lot of things went right and we were just cool, we were easy.

“He’s very down to earth, very cool. We flowed mehn, we flowed.”
