Rahama Sadau Speaks Out On Alleged Police Arrest, Jail Sentence

June 04, 2024
Post

Kannywood actress Rahama Sadau has reacted to reports making rounds that she has been arrested by the police and sentenced to jail over her recent outfit.

The actress who has been in the news recently over the controversy generated by the gown she wore in a recent photo shared on her social media page denied been invited by the police nor sentenced to jail by any court.

She, therefore, urged people to stop spreading fake news about her and appreciated those who have reached out to ensure she’s doing well.

She wrote on her Twitter account on Tuesday: “I have received many messages that I have been arrested and sentenced to jail earlier today. I have no idea where the news emanated from. I’m therefore calling on people to stop spreading FAKE and unfounded stories.”

“I haven’t received any police invitation or court trial. I’m reiterating this to well wishers that I’m SAFE and FINE and I have never received any legal trial for whatever reason. To those trying to bank on this issue to create an unrest, Please give it a rest..!!!”

“It’s indeed a hard time for me.”

“This is not the right time for FAKE NEWS. Much love to those who reached out, I’m grateful.”

It will be recalled that Sadau has already apologized and deleted the sultry image which initially caused the controversy but the matter is yet to die down.

The Kaduna-born actress has been suspended over the same outfit by the Northern filmmakers under the umbrella of Motion Picture Practitioners Association of Nigeria, MOPPAN after they considered her dressing provocative.
