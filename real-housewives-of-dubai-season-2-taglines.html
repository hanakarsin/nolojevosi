The 'RHODubai' Ladies Throw a Proper Amount of Shade in Their Season 2 Taglines

There will drama aplenty in the sophomore season, including a feud between Caroline S. and Sara.

By Sheridan Singleton

May 31 2024, Published 3:54 p.m. ET

The youngest franchise in Bravo's Real Housewives universe is back! That's right, the ladies from The Real Housewives of Dubai are bringing the heat.

The cast has changed slightly thanks to the exit of Nina Ali, but luckily enough, there's a new housewife joining the RHODubai ranks. With the June 2 season premiere, what are the new taglines from the RHODubai cast?

RHODubai is composed of Caroline Brooks, Caroline Stanbury, Chanel Ayan, , Sara Al Madani, and newcomer Taleen Marie. The new season trailer showed that there will be drama aplenty in the sophomore season with a sneak peek into a feud between Caroline S. and Sara.

Caroline Brooks

Tagline: “I may be building glass houses, but you’ll never see me crack.”

After a tumultuous first season, Caroline B. is back and stronger than ever, at least according to her tagline. Season 1 ended with a clear improvement in the relationship with her ex-husband, so hopefully that means she's able to focus on dealing with the other housewives.

Caroline Stanbury

Tagline: “The only thing old about me, darling, is my money.”

If there is one housewife on RHODubai who won't back down when challenged by her fellow cast members, it's Caroline. And if there is one thing to know about people who come from "old money," it's that they have no problem flaunting their wealth. And she's flaunting it well, especially with her recent facelift.

Chanel Ayan

Tagline: “If you’re jealous of me, that’s OK, honey. I would be, too.”

Chanel has shared that she and Caroline S. have made amends In a May 2024 interview with Us Weekly, she commented on Caroline S.'s facelift, saying, "It’s cute, it’s pretty, she looks fabulous, and I cannot be upset. Everything I was upset with her died with that face, the old one.” Given that she has a new makeup line coming out, it makes sense that Chanel would want to get rid of distractions like their old fight.

Lesa Milan

Tagline: “The desert might be hot, but I always find the shade.”

This tagline is very appropriate for Lesa when considering the shade she threw at her fellow housewife Chanel while on the Two Ts in a Pod podcast, where Lesa essentially said that Chanel was two-faced. Given that Chanel said their friendship was finished during the trailer, it seems as if things get pretty heated between the two former friends.

Sara Al Madani

Tagline: “In the land of excess, I find my riches within.”

It looks like Sara is entangled in a conflict with Caroline S., at least according to the season trailer when she calls Sara a hypocrite. While fans will have to wait to understand what happened, it is interesting that Sara also talks about her money. Perhaps this is a new money versus old money situation?

Taleen Marie

Tagline: “Take it from me. If you know how to stay chill, you’ll never get burned.”

As the newcomer in the cast, Taleen Marie seems to be coming into the group with a more levelheaded approach, which is a smart move for someone entering this particular group dynamic. Hopefully, she was able to maintain.
