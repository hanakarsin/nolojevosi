Iran Alleges Who Should Be Blamed For War

Post

Iranian leader, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei has revealed who is to be held responsible for the ongoing crisis between Russia and Ukraine.

He stated that the conflict can only be contained when the root cause is discovered.

He identified the perpetrators of the war as Western powers.

Khamenei said this on Tuesday while making the position of his country on the ongoing war between Russia and Ukraine known.

He said: “The “mafia regime” of the U.S has created many crises around the globe, adding that these crises come in the form of the creation of ISIL (ISIS), and meddling in other nations’ affairs by forcing regime changes and installing pro-West politicians”

He blamed the United States and other western countries for the war while maintaining that western countries cannot be trusted.

“Support by Western governments for administrations and politicians that have been installed by them is a mirage.” he said.
