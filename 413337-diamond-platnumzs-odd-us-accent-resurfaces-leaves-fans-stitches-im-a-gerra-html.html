Wasafi Records owner and renowned musician Diamond Platnumz has amused fans after sharing a video of himself faking an American accent.

In the shared video, the Jeje singer can be heard talking about what drives him and why people call him the lion.

He shared the video on his Instagram page:

"You know how we do men! The reason why they call me Simba is because I'm a tough and aggressive person. I'm a go gerra. Wha-ever I wan, a go gerrit man. That's the lion's spirit. I'm hot. Wha-ever i want I'm gonna hunt it. Can come straight to your face and take it (sic),'' he said.
