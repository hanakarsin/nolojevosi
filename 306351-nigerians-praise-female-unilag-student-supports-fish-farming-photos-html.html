These days, some women have built the reputation of wanting money but not willing to work for it.

Well, that is not the case of a young Gloria who runs a fish farming business.

According to Abimbola who shared the story, Gloria is a graduate of Mathematics from Olabisi Onabanjo University (OOU) and also a masters student at the University of Lagos.

To fund her education and pay her bills, the young lady buys several fish, grows them to a big size, smokes them, and then sells to customers.
