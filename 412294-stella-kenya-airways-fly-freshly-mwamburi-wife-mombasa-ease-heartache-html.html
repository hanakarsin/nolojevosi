Stella: Kenya Airways to Fly Freshley Mwamburi, Wife to Mombasa to Ease Heartache

Legendary musician Freshley Mwamburi will find a new opportunity to heal from the painful heartbreak that was inflicted on him by his lover Stella after Kenya Airways offered to take him on a Mombasa vacation.

Mwamburi became a sensation after his hit song Stella Wangu which was based on a true-life experience as the singer emptied his coffers funding her lifestyle in Japan only for her to reciprocate his kindness with betrayal.

While announcing that Stella would not disembark from KQ on Monday, May 17, another clear indication that she had eaten the musician's 'fare', the airline said it would fly Mwamburi and his wife to Mombasa so that he could heal from the pain meted on him.

"We can now confirm that Stella won't be coming home this year due to travel restrictions. We are “short” of words. However, we'll be happy to deliver some gifts to her via KQ Cargo. As for the one and only Mr FreshlyMwamburi, we are thrilled that he will be flying with us to Mombasa with his new love to ease his heartache," the statement from KQ indicated.

Many lauded Kenya Airways for their offer to the musician saying he deserved a breather while others joked they were disappointed that Stella did not come back home to Mwamburi.

May 17 memes

As earlier reported, Kenyans marked May 17 of every year into their calendar, which is a reminder of how Stella played Mwamburi dirty after he sacrificed everything so she could get an education abroad.

On the 29th anniversary of the song, Netizens flooded social media with hilarious memes and videos to mark the fateful day that Mwamburi will never forget.

Some hilariously mentioned that Stella became the first lady to 'eat' fare sent to her by a man, and some ladies urged others to keep the culture going. Lol.
