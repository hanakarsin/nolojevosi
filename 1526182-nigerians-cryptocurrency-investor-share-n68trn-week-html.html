Cryptocurrency investors in Nigeria and other countries recorded massive gains of N148.9 billion(N68.59trn) between March 12 and March 19, 2023.

This happened as the value of Bitcoin jumped from $20,507.69 to $28,197.25 between March 12 and March 19, data from Marketcap shows.

The increase in Bitcoin's value helped push the market capitalization from $395,849,009,604 to $592,142,194,343, resulting in massive profits for investors.

This surge was especially important for Nigerian investors, who have been actively participating in the cryptocurrency market, given the country's economic uncertainties.
