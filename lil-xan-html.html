Lil Xan - Age, Family, Bio

March 12, 2024
Post

Rapper who built a fan base as an unsigned artist using platforms like SoundCloud and YouTube. He blew up after the release of his hit "Betrayed" in August 2017. He's also known for singles "Slingshot," "Been Bout It," and "Who Are You."

Before Fame

He was raised in Redlands, California.

Trivia

He is affiliated with the Low Gang collective, also featuring $teven Cannon and Aris Ray.

Family Life

His real name is Diego Leanos. His mother's name is Candy. He began dating Annie Smith in 2019. TikTok star gabbytookthese later became his girlfriend. He briefly dated Noah Cyrus in 2018. He has since begun dating Noelle Hear and got a tattoo of her name on his neck.

Associated With

He was featured with Lucki on the song "Xan Man."
