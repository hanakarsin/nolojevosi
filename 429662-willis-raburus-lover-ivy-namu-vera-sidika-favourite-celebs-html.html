Willis Raburu's lover Ivy Namu has disclosed that Vera Sidika is one of her most favourite celebs.

The mother of one is celebrating her birthday week and went on her Instagram Stories to share some of her beloved Libra celebs.

These are celebrities born in the Libra astrological sign, meaning between September 23 and October 23.

Ivy recognised some of her favourite libra celebs, noting she felt it was just right since it was her birthday week.

She named some international celebs, including Eminem, Cardi B and Amber Rose.

She certainly saved the best for last, showing her admiration of socialite Vera Sidika with a stunning photo of the soon-to-be mum in a beautiful dress.

"And finally this pretty mama @queenveebosset," she wrote, adding a love eyes emoji.

Vera's class and personality

Vera has gotten a lot of love from Kenyans, especially as she approaches the end of her pregnancy.

The beauty is known for her extravagant life and no-nonsense persona, which not many can pull off- especially in this social media age.

She has been at the top for so many years, even after moving to Mombasa.

Mauzo celebrates Vera's birthday

A couple of weeks back, Mombasa-based singer Brown Mauzo celebrated his socialite lover Vera in a beautiful post as she turned a year older.
