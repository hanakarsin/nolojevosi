- She described the matriarch who turned 110 as an amazing, special and loving person to her and their family

Shirley Pheladi Ramosepele posted a lovely message on Facebook celebrating her grandmother's amazing 110th birthday.

She celebrated her granny on her special day by saying how much she and her family love her caring spirit, sense of humour and the love she has for her family.

The lovely lady shared the inspirational post in the social media platform group called the #ImStaying group.

"#ImStaying our granny is officially 110 years old today. We love her caring spirit, her sense of humour and the love she has for family and friends. She is indeed a blessing to us and one of a kind. We thank God for keeping her for us," she wrote.
