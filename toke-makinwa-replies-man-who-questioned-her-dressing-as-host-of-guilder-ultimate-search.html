Toke Makinwa Replies Man Who Questioned Her Dressing As Host Of Guilder Ultimate Search

May 16, 2024
Post

Awarding-winning media personality, Toke Makinwa, has replied a man who questioned her outfit while hosting the popular reality show, Gulder Ultimate Search on Sunday.

Naija News earlier reported that the organisers of the Nigerian reality television adventure show, Gulder Ultimate Search unveiled Toke Makinwa as their anchor.

Reacting to her appearance while hosting the show, a fan named Seyi Temitope questioned the media personality turned vlogger about her outfit.

According to the fan, the 36-year-old TV Host was overdressed for the such setting.

Replying the man, Toke asked the man to focus on her hosting skills and not her physical appearance, as  she’s not a contestant and is allowed to dress the way she wants.

Meanwhile recall, the organizers of show, taking to their social media page, announced media personality, Toke Makinwa as the show’s new host.

The post reads; “It’s about time! The age of craftsmanship is upon us and @tokemakinwais ready for the journey. Welcome to the #GulderUltimateSearch,” the video’s caption read.

Confirming the announcement on her personal IG account, Makinwa also shared a video with her 4.7 million followers.

“The council of Elders called and I answer. The age of Craftmanship is finally upon us.

“My name is Toke Makinwa. I am the anchor of the Gulder Ultimate Search – The Age of Craftmanship. Now let the adventures begin… see you at the jungle,” she said.
