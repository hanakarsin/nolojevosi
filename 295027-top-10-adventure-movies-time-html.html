Adventure movies have a special place in everyone’s DVD collection because sometimes you need to engross yourself in a tale of epic proportions. Adventure films can never go out of style and will keep the audience excited with the improvement of graphics. These films transport us to various places and times from regular life to an exciting new world that has diverse possibilities.

Some people best define an adventure movie in terms of how well the film illustrates the pursuit of luck and exaltation. If you're a thrill-seeking, adrenaline-obsessed movie-goer, these are the top adventure movies you have to see.

1. Jurassic Park

IMDB rating: 8.1/10

Jurassic Park is a 1993 release directed by Steven Spielberg. The movie combines the aspects of adventure with thriller and Science-Fiction to keep the lovers glued to their screens. The film takes place in a Jurassic park during a preview tour by various individuals from all corners of the earth. During the visit, there is a power breakdown that in turn leads to allowing the cloned dinosaur to roam freely. With the intensifying and massive advancements in the field of science and technology, an entrepreneur successfully created an island full of cloned living dinosaurs. The adventurous trip turns chaotic as everyone is out to fight for their lives amidst the freely roaming dinosaurs.
