Omotola Jalade Shares Bedroom Photo With Her Husband To Mark His Birthday

Post

Veteran Nollywood actress, Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde on Thursday shared a bedroom photo of herself and husband, Matthew Ekeinde to mark his birthday and 27 years of being married.

The mother of four shared a shirtless photo of her husband and herself in bed; with a visible tattoo on her husband’s arm ‘Omosexy’, which is one of the actress’ nicknames.

Sharing the photo on her Instagram, the actress called her husband, ‘HoneyBoy’ adding that their last kiddo is now 21.

Her post reads: “Happy birthday my Forever HoneyBoy. And…Happy Anniversary to us.

“27 years and we only just about to get into phase 2 ! Last kiddo now 21… no more PG …”

Omotola’s husband is a Nigerian pilot. The couple tied the knot in 1996 and in 2000, they renewed their vows in in mid-air.

The wedding ceremony took place on board Dash 7, an aircraft.

The officiating priest, family members, and a few friends were present at the wedding, which made headlines at the time.

To make the wedding more fun and memorable, Capt. Matthew Ekeinde flew the plane for about 10 minutes. He only left the plane to his co-pilot when it was time to say his vows.

Together, they have four children; Princess Ekeinde, Meraiah Ekeinde, Captain E, and Micheal Ekeinde.
