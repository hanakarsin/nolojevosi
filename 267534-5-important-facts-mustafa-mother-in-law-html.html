5 Important facts that you should know about Mustafa of Mother In Law

February 27, 2024
Post

Mustafa of the mother in law Citizen TV show is the ‘Leso’ wearing character that plays the cook. The Mother in law actor real name is Andrew Muthure, but his friends call him ‘Dru’. He has been acting on Mother in Law for more than ten years now. Initially, before we started seeing Mustafa on TV, he used to be a star doing comedy at Phoenix Theatres. Owing to his immense talent in the theatres, Naomi Kamau scouted him for a job landing his big break. Besides theatre and his role at Mother in Law local Television series, Mustafa has also done several other films.

According to Andrew, his most memorable moment on Tv was when he handled a real gun during a DSTV Africa Magic Originals Film job. Moreover, he also loved cruising around in the different luxury cars on set. Even though Mustafa is a household name, not much about the actor is known to the public. This article dug deeper to know more about the talented comedian, and here we present five facts about Mustafa you probably did not know.

1. Mustafa is not from the Coast

Owing to Mustafa’s accent on Mother In Law series, you probably thought that he was either from the coast or has lived there for a while. Well, that is definitely not true, as Andrew Muthure was born in Kangemi and raised in Kibera slums. According to the talented actor, he only visits the coastal region when on vacation just like the rest of us. When inquired as to how he acquired his Swahili accent, Dru explained that he lived in Tanzania for two years a while back during which his flair for the language as well as his swag got some polishing up.

2. Andrew Muthure is a Marketing degree holder

Given his career, you’d think that Dru pursued an acting degree; however, this is not the case. Mustafa graduated with an undergraduate in Marketing from the African Nazarene University even though he revealed that his heart was always with TV and Radio. Moreover, Mustafa expressed how when he was enrolling for his college studies, there were only two universities offering theatre studies and at the time he had not considered doing acting as a profession. It is also worth noting that Mustafa has never worked as a marketer; however, he explains that his skills as a marketer come in handy when handling presentations and when talking to his audience on TV or radio.

3. Mustafa got his first girlfriend very young

During an interview with one of the big media houses, Mustafa revealed that he started dating quite early while in primary school. His first girlfriend was a form two student when he was still in class eight. According to Dru, He loved dating older girls when he was young. Presently, Mustafa is in a committed relationship and is blessed with an adorable eight-year-old daughter.

4. Andrew was an assistant director

Mustafa of mother in law used to be the assistant director of Makutano Junction Local Tv series. During that time, he learnt of the Mother in law on-going auditions, and he decided to give it a try, and he was picked as part of the cast. According to Dru, the Mother in Law actors and actresses are jovial talented individuals that he loves working with.

5. Mustafa Hustled long for his Radio Job

The fifth fact about Mustafa is that his job at Hot 96 FM did not fall on his laps and he had to hustle for close to 12 years for it. According to the popular actor, he went for several radio interviews without any success when finally one day he got a call back from Hot 96 Fm. Mustafa started his radio job in 2015 after emerging the best candidate in a second round interview.
