Yet another popular NTV presenter opens up about her struggle with endometriosis

May 07, 2024
Post

- The internet was sent into a frenzy after former The Trend presenter Ciru Muriuki shared her struggle with endometriosis

- She posted a post-operation photo of herself that saddened her fans

- Her loyal viewers flooded her timeline as they realized that she had been suffering silently with the condition

Former The Trend panelist Ciru Muriuki is a bubbly and charming young woman who has an interesting and unique take on life.

The popular TV screen siren has a wide following and has never been afraid of speaking whatever's on her mind.

Just recently, the curvy dread head exited NTV and joined K24 after co-hosting The Trend for quite a while.

Kenyans woke up to a bitter surprise on Sunday, December 24 when the TV queen revealed that she had gone under the knife due to endometriosis-related complications.

In a touching Instagram post, she thanked Njambi Koikai for creating a platform for women to speak out about their battle scars.

READ ALSO: Ciru Muriuki pens emotional message to fans after leaving the Trend show

Ciru had kept her disease under wraps but decided to speak up in a bid to encourage her fans and ladies suffering from the same predicament.

"@jahmbykoikai you're a rock star for bringing awareness to endometriosis. So many of us are suffering in silence, faced with physicians who don't really care. I remember one who insinuated that my pelvic pain was caused by an STD. SURELY." She wrote.

"Anyways, I had a laparoscopic procedure yesterday to remove a large hemorrhagic ovarian cyst. I'm a little sore, but on the mend. We need a cure. We do." She continued.
