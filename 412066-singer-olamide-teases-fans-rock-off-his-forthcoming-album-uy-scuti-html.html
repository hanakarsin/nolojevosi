Nigerian musicians have proven to the world countless times that they are here not for games, but for work.

This can be explained by the number of massive hits Naija musicians have been dropping over the recent past.

The new act to claim a position at the top is none other than singer Olamide whose new release has been causing ripples in the African music scene.

Olamide recently shared the audio and visuals for his latest jam Rock, the first single from his forthcoming album, UY Scuti.

The ten-track LP, Olamide’s third new music project in just over a year, is set for release on June 14 through a partnership between his own YBNL Nation imprint and US indie powerhouse, EMPIRE

The video for Rock was directed by renowned Nigerian director Clarence Peters, takes viewers inside a noir vision of Lagos, starring Olamide and dancer Soliat Bada, as flirtatious lovers plotting a rendezvous.

Rising Nigerian beatmaker Eskeez, who handles the lion’s share of production on ‘UY Scuti’, produced the intimate, mid-tempo Afrobeats scorcher.

The song is saying my lady doesn’t have to do too much to impress me — I love you already, Olamidé says.

It’s expressing what a guy would sing to a girl to give her confidence, according to the star.

''I am telling the women out there that, if a man truly loves you, you don’t have to go overboard to please him.'' he said.
