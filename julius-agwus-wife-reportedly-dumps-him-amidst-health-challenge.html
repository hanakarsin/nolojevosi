Julius Agwus Wife Reportedly Dumps Him Amidst Health Challenge

March 17, 2024
Post

Nigerian comedian, Julius Agwu’s marriage to his wife, Ibiere Maclayton has reportedly crashed.

Naija News understands that the couple who have been married for over 14 years fell apart over irreconsible differences.

The comedian had a challenging time when he fell ill in 2015 and has since continued to maintain a very low profile.

Julius Agwu is said to be recuperating and everyone felt his wife was taking care of him until she reportedly traveled abroad with her kids and only shares their photos on Instagram, having reverted to her maiden name.

According to the Punch, an Inside source close to family said, “She took care of him for sometimes but there were cracks in the union. Maybe she got tired of the current situation—cash flow wasn’t as when Agwu was stupendously rich and healthy. She must have been tired of the situation and left Agwu who isn’t as fit as before.’’

In 2020, Julius Agwu attempted to resuscitate his career by staging another edition of ‘Crack ya ribs,’ his annual comedy concert across the country and disclosed his plan to celebrate 25 years in comedy. Unfortunately, that dream didn’t see the light of day.

The Comedian and his wife got married on May 31 2008 and are blessed with two kids, Zahra & Zadok. In 2020, the couple celebrated their 12th wedding anniversary.
