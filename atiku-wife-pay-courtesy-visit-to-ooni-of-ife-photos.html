Atiku, Wife Pay Courtesy Visit To Ooni Of Ife (Photos)

April 01, 2024
Post

The presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar and his wife, Titi Abubakar on Tuesday visited the Ooni of Ife, Oba Adeyeye Enitan Ogunwusi.

The vice presidential candidate of the main opposition party, Ifeanyi Okowa (Governor of Delta State) was also in attendance.

Naija News gathered that the former vice president alongside other members of entourage visited the monarch at his palace in Ile-Ife, Osun State.

The wife of the PDP presidential candidate in a post on her Twitter account on Tuesday said she and her husband paid a courtesy visit to the Ooni of Ife.

At the time of filing this report, the details of Atiku’s meeting with the monarch ahead of the 2023 presidential election has not been disclosed.

Atiku is hoping to take over from President Muhammadu Buhari at the end of his tenure in May 2023.

The PDP presidential candidate would be contesting against the flagbearer of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu, the Labour Party (LP) presidential candidate, Peter Obi, Rabiu Kwankwaso of the New Nigeria Peoples Party (NNPP) and several other presidential candidates in 2023.
